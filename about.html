Our Story

Coastal Escape Rentals was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide travelers with unforgettable vacation experiences along the stunning California coast. What started as a small family business with just a handful of properties has grown into a leading rental agency, offering a curated selection of beachfront homes, cozy cottages, and luxurious estates.

Our commitment to exceptional customer service and meticulously maintained properties sets us apart. We believe that every vacation should be a memorable escape, and we strive to make that a reality for each of our guests. From the moment you book your stay to the time you check out, we're dedicated to ensuring your comfort and satisfaction. We've expanded our offerings to include personalized concierge services, local activity recommendations, and enhanced cleaning protocols to meet the evolving needs of our guests.
